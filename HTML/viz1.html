Overview

We also analyzed the length of games over the course of 3 months between the crewmates and the imposters. It is important to note that there can be between seven and nine crewmates and only one to three imposters. Therefore, a crewmate has a larger probability of having a longer game. We expected to see a trend similar to this theory over the course of November, December and January. However, in looking at the graphs, one can see that the chart analyzing game length by month for crewmates vs imposters is fairly similar, and also mirrors similar trends. January shows the shortest average game length for both imposters and crewmates at 470s and 557s respectively, whereas November showed the longest average game length for both at 682s and 677s.
