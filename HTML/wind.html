Latitude vs. Wind Speed

Analysis

This graph shows the relationship between latitude and the wind speed recorded in each city when the data was collected.

Wind speed was found to be lower in cities near the equator, and highest in cities that were farthest north from the equator, with a couple extremes being greater than 30 mph in some northern cities.
