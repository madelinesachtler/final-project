Conclusion

Our final thoughts on this project mainly revolve around game strategy, as all three of us have become avid players in the recent months. Our goal was to identify any potential factors in the game that led to a larger outcome of winning either as a Crewmate or as an imposter. What we were able to uncover after extensive machine learning is that game length plays a significant factor in the outcome of a game. We then built visualizations displaying this factor against the outcome of the game and included other factors as well that were also slightly important. We saw that the longer that a game lasts while remaining in session means that the number of Crewmates is decreasing and the imposter or imposters, have not been ejected yet. Therefore, we expected to see an increase in number of wins of a game during the early stages as opposed to the later stages where the ratio of Crewmates to Imposters is larger. We also deduced that perhaps there is a switch in priorities towards the end of the game, where identifying the imposter is of less importance, and completing all tasks before everyone dies is of more importance.
